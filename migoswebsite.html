M I G O S

Who is Migos?

Migos is a rap group that consist of three members, Offset, Quavo and Takeoff. Formed in 2009 the tree memebers not only grew up together but they are also related, Quavo being Takeoff's uncle and Offset being Quavo's cousin. They originate from Georgia where 'migos' meant an abandoned house used as a trap house. The recognition came from their hit Versace(2013).Recently their hit Bad and Bougie(2017) made them known internationally.
